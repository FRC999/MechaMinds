Quick brushing reminder

Brush twice a day for two minutes with fluoride toothpaste. Angle the bristles toward the gumline and use gentle circles.

Quick flossing reminder

Clean between teeth once a day. Slide the floss gently under the gumline and curve it into a C-shape around each tooth.
