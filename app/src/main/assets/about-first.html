About FIRST ®
FIRST is a nonprofit that helps young people build science, technology, engineering, and teamwork skills through fun robotics programs. MechaRams is proud to be part of FIRST Robotics Competition.
Visit the FIRST website
What does FIRST stand for?
FIRST stands for For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology.
What is FIRST Robotics Competition?
FIRST Robotics Competition, often called FRC, is a program where high school teams design, build, program, and compete with large robots while also doing outreach and community impact.
What age groups can join FIRST?
FIRST has programs for many ages, from early elementary students through high school. That means kids can grow with robotics year after year.
What do students learn in FIRST?
Students learn engineering, coding, design, strategy, communication, leadership, teamwork, and problem solving. They also build confidence by creating something real.
Why is FIRST important?
FIRST makes STEM exciting and hands-on. It helps students discover what they can do, explore careers, and see that science and technology can be creative, meaningful, and fun.
Who are the MechaRams?
MechaRams is FRC Team 999 from Cheshire High School. We build robots, mentor younger students, and spread STEM in our community.
